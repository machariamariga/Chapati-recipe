Here is all you need

15 chapatis, Serves 7

Ingredient Quantity
Wheat flour A kilo
Warm Water One litre
salt Half a teaspoon
Sugar A teaspoon
Cooking oil half a cup

Now that you know what you need here is the process of it all

  1. Mix the warm water and the flour into a consistent dough that is not sticky and also not too dry
  2. After the dough is ready, cut it out to smaller rollable pieces into sort of small flat pieces
  3. once that is done, it is time for the rolling which in my opinion is the most fun part
  4. After rolling have your pan hot and ready to get cooking
  5. First dry the rolled chapati without oil first but as soon as it starts showing brown speck, whip out the oil and add a little oil frying each side until a consistent brown colour appears

GET COOKING!!!!
